How To Make Salad

On this page we are going to take a look at how to make a delicious Salad

Ingredients

Below are ingredients to make salad:
  • Carrot
  • carbage
  • beans
  • corn
  • mayonnaise

Instructions

Below are instructions to make salad:
  1. Cut the carrots
  2. cut/slice the carbage and it together with the carrot
  3. mix the beans and the corn
  4. Add all the ingredients together, then mix it with the mayo
